Font Mannerisms Folio

A look into the typeface, Baskerville using a Font Mannerisms Folio. This project entailed receiving a random font to discuss the history and characteristics of popular typefaces. Throughout this folio, the typeface Baskerville is explored for the mood it sets and the characterisitcs that define it.
